Cristiano Ronaldo CR7

The Madira rocket

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus and captains the Portugal national team

Here's a time line of CR7 life:

  • 1985 - born in Hospital Dr. Nélio Mendonça, Funchal, Portugal
  • 1997 - at age 12, he went on a trial with Sporting CP, who signed him for a fee of £1,500.
  • 2001 - CR7 joined professional football
  • 2003 - Ronaldo joined Manchester United from Sporting CP.
  • 2007/08 - he became the only player in the last 25 years to win both the "FIFA World Player of the Year" and the "Young Player of the Year" awards in the same season
  • 2009 - Manchester United accepted an offer of £80 million from Real Madrid, making Ronaldo the most expensive player in the world
  • 2018 - Ronaldo was signed by Italian club Juventus for a €100 transfer fee (plus additional €12 million in other fees
